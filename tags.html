Meditation

Meditation will help relax the mind and bring your body back to center. Start with at least 5 minutes a day and build up.

Health Benefits

People use the practice to develop other beneficial habits and feelings, such as a positive mood and outlook, self-discipline, healthy sleep patterns, and even increased pain tolerance.
